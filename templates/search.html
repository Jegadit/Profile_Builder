    • SEARCH OUR FACULTY

    When conducting a search for faculty members in a college, a systematic classification process is employed to identify the most suitable candidates. This classification involves assessing their academic credentials, including degrees and areas of expertise. Additionally, their teaching experience and effectiveness are evaluated to ensure they can deliver quality instruction. Research accomplishments and contributions to their field are also considered to gauge their expertise and potential for advancing knowledge. Finally, personal attributes like communication skills and a passion for teaching are examined to ensure a well-rounded and dedicated faculty member.
